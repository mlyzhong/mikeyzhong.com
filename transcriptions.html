TRANSCRIPTIONS
Every now and then, I transcribe some music that I like. I use Amazing Slow Downer to slow down clips and raise the pitch of sub-bass notes. For melodic transcriptions, I record midi on Ableton and fix up sheet music using musescore. For drum beats, I use The Funklet.
